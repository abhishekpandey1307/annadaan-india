Our Mission & Story

Turning excess food into meals, dignity, and hope. Welcome to the heart of Annadaan.

Our Mission

At Annadaan, our mission is to eliminate food waste and fight hunger by connecting surplus food from individuals and events with NGOs ready to distribute it. Every donated plate is a life touched, a hunger eased, and a step closer to a compassionate world.
